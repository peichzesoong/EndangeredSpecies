About Us

Our Mission

For Nature, For People, Forever

To stop the degradation of the earth’s natural environment and to build a future in which humans live in harmony with nature by:

  • Conserving the world’s biological diversity.
  • Ensuring that the use of renewable natural resources is sustainable.
  • Promoting the reduction of pollution and wasteful consumption.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, ...organization name... is part of WWF, the international conservation organisation.

People all over the world are becoming more aware of the growing crisis of nature loss. We are increasingly aware that nature is our life support system, and that no one will be immune to the consequences of its failure.

As part of WWF, an independent conservation organization active in nearly 100 countries, we are working to sustain the natural world for the benefit of people and wildlife.

WWF urgently aims to conserve and preserve natural environments, avoid mass extinction of species, and make the way we create and consume sustainable, working with a wide range of partners ranging from individuals and communities to businesses and governments.

Our Projects

The projects that our organisation does are focused on six major goals, which are forests, oceans, wildlife, food, climate and energy and freshwater. From donations to conservation, our projects strive to achieve our mission.
