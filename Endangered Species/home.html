Who are we?

As a group of animal enthusiasts, our team, MMF.org, Mother earth Maintenance Fund, is made up of zoologists, veterinarians, mammal lovers and v-pet collectors. We love every animals, and we put our love into action by founding this organization under indirect support from WWF, and we aimed to rescue every endangered animals in the world.
